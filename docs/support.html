Getting Started

What is StudioSnap?

StudioSnap is an AI-powered product photography app. Upload a photo of your product and choose from curated studio-style backgrounds — the AI removes your original background and composites your product into a professional scene in seconds.

Results are optimised for selling on marketplaces like Etsy, eBay, and Vinted.

How do I use StudioSnap?

Using StudioSnap is simple:

  • Tap Add Product Photos and select up to 10 images from your gallery
  • Pick a background style that matches your brand
  • Choose optional enhancements (shadow, reflection) and export size
  • Tap Generate — results are ready in 10–30 seconds per photo
  • Save or share your styled images directly from the Results screen
Can I try StudioSnap for free?

Yes! New users receive a free trial photo on sign-up. The result will include a watermark, but it lets you see exactly how your product looks in a styled scene before you commit to buying credits.

What types of products work best?

StudioSnap works across a wide range of product categories:

Clothing Jewellery Homeware Cosmetics Food and more

For best results, photograph your product against a plain or contrasting background with good lighting before uploading.

Credits & Pricing

How does the credit system work?

1 credit = 1 styled photo. If you generate a batch of 5 photos, that costs 5 credits. Credits are deducted when generation completes successfully.

Do credits expire?

No. Once purchased, your credits never expire. Use them at your own pace.

How do I buy more credits?

Tap the credits icon in the top-right corner of the home screen, or go to Settings → Get More Credits. Credit packs are available in three tiers — choose what suits your volume.

Purchases are processed securely by the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

What happens if a photo fails to generate?

If any photo in your batch fails, the credit for that photo is automatically refunded to your account. You'll see a notification like "2 of 5 images failed — 2 credits refunded" on the Results screen.

How do I restore previous purchases?

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, you can restore your credits:

  • Go to Settings → Restore Purchases
  • Sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used when purchasing
  • Your credits will be restored automatically

If that doesn't work, email us at middleton.apps@gmail.com with your order confirmation and we'll sort it out.

Can I get a refund?

Purchases are handled by Apple or Google, so refunds must be requested through them:

  • iOS: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Android: Contact Google Play support via the Play Store app

If you believe credits were deducted incorrectly due to a technical issue, email us directly and we'll investigate.

Styles & Generating

What background styles are available?

StudioSnap includes 18 curated studio styles, including:

Clean White Warm Linen Marble Luxe Botanical Garden Dark Moody Neon Pop Pastel Dream Rustic Wood Silk Velvet and more…

New styles are added regularly. Styles are organised by category so you can quickly find the right vibe for your product.

How many photos can I process at once?

You can upload and process up to 10 photos per batch. All photos in a batch are styled with the same background and settings you've chosen, making it fast to create a consistent product listing.

How long does generation take?

Generation typically takes 10–30 seconds per photo. Please keep the app open during processing — closing it may interrupt the generation.

What export sizes are available?

StudioSnap exports photos optimised for the most popular selling platforms:

  • Etsy Square — ideal for Etsy listings
  • eBay Landscape — optimised for eBay
  • Vinted Portrait — sized for Vinted
  • Original — keeps your source aspect ratio
Can I add a shadow or reflection?

Yes. Before generating, toggle Add Shadow or Add Reflection in the Options section. These add subtle, realistic depth to your product — great for making it pop against clean backgrounds.

Account & Privacy

Do I need an account to use StudioSnap?

You can try the free trial without an account. To purchase credits and sync your history across devices, you'll need to sign in with Apple or Google — it only takes a few seconds.

Benefits of signing in:

  • Credits sync across all your devices
  • Never lose your generation history
  • Secure and private
What happens to my photos?

Your photos are sent securely to our servers for processing and are deleted immediately after your styled result is generated. We never store, sell, or use your images for any other purpose.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

If you have remaining credits you'd like to discuss before deleting, email us first at middleton.apps@gmail.com.

Where is my generation history stored?

Generated images are saved to your device and are accessible via the History tab in the app. If you delete a session within the app, the images are removed from local storage. Images are not stored on our servers after generation.

Troubleshooting

Generation failed — what should I do?

If a generation fails, any credits used are automatically refunded. Common causes:

  • Network issue — check your internet connection and try again
  • App in background — keep the app open during generation
  • Image format — use JPG or PNG files for best results

If failures persist, email us at middleton.apps@gmail.com and we'll investigate.

My credits aren't showing up after purchase

First, try Settings → Restore Purchases to sync your purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same account used for the purchase.

If credits still don't appear after a few minutes, email us at middleton.apps@gmail.com with a screenshot of your purchase confirmation and we'll add them manually.

The app is crashing or behaving unexpectedly

Try these steps in order:

  • Force-close and reopen the app
  • Check for an app update in the App Store or Google Play
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app (your credits are linked to your account and will be restored)

If the issue persists, please email middleton.apps@gmail.com with your device model and iOS/Android version so we can investigate.

Can't sign in with Apple or Google

For Apple sign-in issues, make sure you're signed into iCloud on your device (Settings → Apple ID). For Google sign-in, ensure a Google account is added to your device.

If the problem continues, email us at middleton.apps@gmail.com.
